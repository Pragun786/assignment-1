Comicstaan Winner "Samay Raina"

Stand-up Comedian | Chess Enthusiast | Content Creator

Some key achievements
  • Gained national recognition by co-winning Amazon Prime Video's comedy competition alongside Aakash Gupta.
  • Started performing at open mics in Pune in 2017, eventually opening for comedians like Anirban Dasgupta and Abhishek Upmanyu before moving to Mumbai to pursue comedy full-time.
  • Creator and host of this YouTube-based talent show blending humor and performance, which later expanded into a mobile app and OTT platform.
  • Popularized chess in India during the COVID-19 lockdown by streaming games with top players like Viswanathan Anand and Vidit Gujrathi.
